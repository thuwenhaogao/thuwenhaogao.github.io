Biography

Mr. Wenhao Gao is currently pursuing his Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering at Tsinghua University. He received his Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Chongqing University in 2022 and earned a Master's degree from National University of Singapore in 2023. His research primarily focuses on the offshore wind power optimization.
